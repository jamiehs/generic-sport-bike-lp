Generic Sport Bike

A 3D Model meticulously created with a professional workflow in mind.

This 3D model was created in response to many purchasers needs for a generic looking sport bike—studios tend to anonymize models used in production.

Games like Burnout, and T.V. Shows such as The Works tend to remove graphics, choose unused paint colors, and even go as far as mirroring and warping geometry to obfuscate the models being used. Sometimes the even use fictional brands.

Seeing my bike models used on cable T.V. has taught me one thing: No one cares that it's a <insert brand here> bike, just that it's a bike. That's where the Generic Sport Bike comes in.

Available on the:
  • Original facade, a design born from trends at the time.
  • A reliable representation of a sport bike.
  • Meticulously modeled with subdivision in mind. It's composed of only quadrangles and loops keep the edge detail.
  • Broken into over 300 objects and hierarchically organized.
  • Extremely easy to repaint or apply decals.

When creating this model, I wanted to build something that was both useful and amazingly detailed. After having seen how my bike models were used in the past on television, I got a good idea of how much detail is desired, and how much detail is too much.

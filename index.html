Generic Sport Bike

A 3D Model meticulously created with a professional workflow in mind.

This 3D model was created in response to many purchasers needs for a generic looking sport bike. Since copyright is such a complex issue with a combination of abuse, confusion, and mixed interests, production houses tend to anonymize models used in production.

Games like Burnout, and T.V. Shows such as The Works tend to remove graphics, choose unused paint colors, and even go as far as mirroring and warping geometry to obfuscate the models being used.

Seeing my bike models used on cable T.V. has taight me one thing: No one cares that it's a <insert brand here> just that it's a bike. That's where the Generic Sport Bike comes in.

Available on:
  • Original facade, a design born from trends at the time.
  • A reliable representation of a sport bike.
  • Meticulously modeled with subdivision in mind. It's composed of only quadrangles and loops keep the edge detail.
  • Broken into over 300 objects and hierarchically organized.
  • Extremely easy to repaint or apply decals.
